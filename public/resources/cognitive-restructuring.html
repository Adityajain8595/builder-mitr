Cognitive Restructuring

Restructuring means noticing unhelpful thoughts and gently replacing them with more balanced alternatives.

Steps

  1. Write the situation and emotion (0–100%).
  2. Capture the automatic thought.
  3. List evidence for and against the thought.
  4. Craft a balanced alternative.
  5. Re‑rate the emotion.

Tips

  • Look for thinking styles: all‑or‑nothing, catastrophizing, mind‑reading.
  • Ask: What would I tell a friend I care about?
  • Small, believable alternatives work best.
