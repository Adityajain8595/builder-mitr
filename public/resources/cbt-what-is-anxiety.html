CBT: What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is a natural alarm system. In CBT we learn how thoughts, feelings, and actions keep anxiety going—and how to break that cycle.

The cycle

  • Trigger → thought ("What if…")
  • Body → racing heart, tension, short breath
  • Behavior → avoidance, reassurance seeking

Skills

  • Slow breathing (4‑4‑4‑4 box breathing)
  • Thought challenging and compassionate reframing
  • Gradual exposure to rebuild confidence
