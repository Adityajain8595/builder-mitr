Box Breathing (4–4–4–4)

Use this simple pattern to calm your nervous system.

  1. Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds.
  2. Hold for 4 seconds.
  3. Exhale through your mouth for 4 seconds.
  4. Hold for 4 seconds. Repeat 4 rounds.

Sit upright, relax shoulders, and breathe into your belly. If dizzy, pause and return to normal breathing.
